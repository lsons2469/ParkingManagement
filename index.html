Fully integrated parking info

The headunit parking interface can be fully skinned and integrated into the navigation system. The parking service displays the closest parking facilities to the current car location, provides information on hours of operation, prices, security, availability of EV charging, etc.

In-car transactions

Drivers are able to not only find parking but also pay for parking directly from the convenience of their car.
